TechTrenders was born in 2018 from a shared vision between journalist Maya Chen and engineer Elias Thorne. Frustrated by shallow headlines, they aimed to bridge the gap between complex engineering and human impact. Operating from a Seattle apartment, they chose the signature #2980b9 blue to symbolize trust and innovation. Their mission was simple: provide "Future Forward News" that identifies where technology is headed, not just where it is. After a viral 2019 report on AI privacy, the site evolved from a side-hustle into a global authority. Today, TechTrenders remains dedicated to clarifying the complex in an ever-evolving digital world.
